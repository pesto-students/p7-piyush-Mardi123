Who are Movie Buffs?

Definition

Movie buff as a person who watches films critically, looking for the underlying themes and design techniques that define a work. These can be folks who take notice of certain acting or directing techniques in addition to those who try and tease out the sociopolitical implications behind the choices made.

Characterstics

Someone who is crazy about movies and would be interested in movies of all genres and time. They know every “unnecessary “ details , including the directors style, hidden meaning, Easter eggs etc. If you can sit through 2001: A Space Odessey without blinking an eye, then you are either a movie buff or dead. Similarly if you get excited everytime a Quetin Tarantino movie is played, then you can consider yourself a movie buff. (Either that or gore turns you on.)

Advantages

Go to a bar, meet a dame and you had seen enough movies to use the right pick up line. Meet the girl and talk to her. You know exactly how to use your charm (Thanks to James Bond) Meet your friends from another place and you know everything (almost everything) about their culture.
